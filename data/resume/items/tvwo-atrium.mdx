VWO (voorbereidend wetenschappelijk onderwijs, or "pre-university secondary education" in English) is the highest variant in the secondary educational system of the Netherlands. The T in (T)VWO stands for tweetalig ("bilingual"), meaning about half of the courses were taught in English. Gained invaluable experience speaking English when staying in host families in Ireland and participating in an exchange programme with Canadian students.
