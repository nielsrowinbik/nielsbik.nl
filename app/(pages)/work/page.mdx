### Stuvia

_Product Manager_

At Stuvia, I joined as the company's first product hire to help build a more user- and product-centric culture. I introduced core product practices including vision and strategy definition, an internally public roadmap, user research, and lightweight discovery processes. Together with our designer, I formalised and began gradually rolling out a new design system to modernise the platform without a risky rebuild. I also led the development of a Next.js-based marketing site and React-based component library to give non-engineering teams more autonomy. My focus has consistently been on creating high-quality user experiences that are informed by research and aligned with long-term product thinking, bringing structure and intent to how the product is shaped and evolved.
